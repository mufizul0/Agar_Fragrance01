Vision & Mission

Our aim is to strengthen general and online marketing to keep Agar-Ator on the top ten list in the global Agar-Ator market. We focus on our Agar Ator Made in Bangladesh considering Bangladeshi Agar Ator is best in the world.

The agar of Bengal is concerned with the 400-year history and tradition of the Muslim aristocracy. The Mission of Agar Fragrance is to re-establish Agar-Ator in the states of the Muslim World. Agar Fragrance is one of the leading companies of the country. This company was established in 2011 not only purpose of business but also for restoration of Muslim aristocracy.

Future Plan of Agar Fragrance : To spread our market from middle-east to the different parts of the world. We are expecting to make our product available to more than 100 countries in the upcoming year Insha Allah.

About Us Agar Fragrance Trusted in world Wide

In Bangladesh there are several private companies related to Agar business. Most of their factories are in the northeast region of the country. One of the leading Agar wood manufacturer & Exporter companies of Bangladesh is Agar Fragrance and its brand name is Doe Empex. This company started its journey on December 2011. It has three main products regarding Agar. Agar Wood Oil Agar Wood Chips and Agar Oud Read More .

Meet Our Organizer

President

Bismillahir Rahmanir Rahim. First of all, I am grateful to Allah the Almighty that He has made me follower of Islam which is His granted religion. I offer my greetings and salutations to the great Prophet Hajrat Muhammed (Sm) who used to love fragrances and encouraged his ummah to use it. And I have been playing the main role of a company that produces Agar oil, Agar Wood Chips, Agar Oud and our brand name is Doe Empex (Made In Bangladesh). I am trying to supply the agar produced in Bangladesh to the world market by maintaining the quality of the product which is the symbol of Muslim nobility. We are always striving to speed up the process of conservation, proper care and production of the precious God-gift natural tree gardens. We believe that with the cooperation of all, the 400-year-old and traditional agar of Bangladesh will be able to occupy the place of the best Agar-Ator in the world again.
