Agar wood is also known as Eaglewood, Aloes wood

There are seventeen species in the genus Aquilaria, large evergreens native to southeast Asia, and nine are known to produce agar wood. In theory Agar wood can be produced from all members; however, until recently it was primarily produced from A. malaccensis.
